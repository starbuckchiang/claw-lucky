# claw-lucky Product Vision

Version: 1.0

---

# Product Vision

claw-lucky is not a traditional online claw machine.

It is an AI-powered lucky experience platform that combines collecting, gifting, personalization, and emotional interaction into a daily habit.

Our goal is to make users feel that every visit brings a little more luck than yesterday.

---

# Mission

Create small moments of happiness through AI, mascots, and lucky experiences.

Every mascot should have a personality.

Every gift should carry a story.

Every wallpaper should become a unique memory.

---

# Long-term Vision

Build the world's most delightful AI lucky companion.

Instead of simply collecting digital items, users build a growing relationship with their mascots and the AI Shopkeeper.

The platform should evolve from:

Lucky Draw

↓

Collection

↓

Gift Redemption

↓

AI Lucky Wallpaper

↓

Daily Lucky Companion

↓

AI Shopkeeper

↓

Community

↓

Digital Lifestyle

---

# Core Experience

Every visit should answer one question:

"What is my luck today?"

Users should feel excited before drawing.

Satisfied after collecting.

Curious when talking with the AI Shopkeeper.

Happy when downloading their personalized wallpaper.

Motivated to return tomorrow.

---

# Product Principles

## 1. Emotional First

Users remember feelings before features.

Every interaction should create positive emotions.

---

## 2. Collectibility

Mascots should feel valuable.

Collections should encourage long-term engagement.

Duplicate mascots should still have meaningful uses.

---

## 3. Daily Habit

The platform should provide new reasons to return every day.

Examples:

- Daily blessing
- Daily lucky wallpaper
- Daily mission
- Daily surprise
- Daily event

---

## 4. AI as a Companion

AI is not just a chatbot.

The AI Shopkeeper is a virtual store manager who knows the mascots, gifts, and users.

The AI should:

- celebrate lucky moments
- encourage users
- generate blessings
- recommend gifts
- guide exploration

---

## 5. Delight over Efficiency

The product is designed to create joy rather than complete tasks quickly.

Animations, sounds, stories, and surprises are encouraged.

---

# Product Pillars

## Lucky

Every interaction should reinforce the feeling of good fortune.

---

## Collection

Mascots become long-term companions instead of disposable rewards.

---

## Personalization

Every user should have a unique experience.

Examples:

- AI blessings
- Personalized wallpaper
- Lucky history
- Future AI conversations

---

## Sharing

The platform should naturally encourage users to share:

- wallpapers
- mascots
- lucky moments
- achievements

without forcing social behavior.

---

## Growth

The platform should continuously unlock new experiences.

Examples:

- New mascot series
- Seasonal events
- AI features
- Collaboration campaigns

---

# AI Vision

AI should enhance emotion rather than replace gameplay.

The AI Shopkeeper should feel:

- warm
- friendly
- encouraging
- playful
- optimistic

Never robotic.

Never cold.

# Success Metrics

The product succeeds when users:

- return every day
- collect mascots voluntarily
- redeem gifts frequently
- create wallpapers regularly
- recommend the platform to friends

---

# What We Will Not Build

The project is NOT intended to become:

- a gambling platform
- a pure e-commerce website
- a generic AI image generator
- a traditional online store

Every new feature should strengthen the lucky experience.
